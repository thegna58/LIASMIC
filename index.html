What do you understand by Mental Health? What are your ideas to bring mental Health Awareness?

Mental health in simple words means the well being of your mind. The mind is a complicated machine with emotions. To have control over our emotions taking care of mental health is important. Mental health can affect our daily behaviour. A healthy mind leads to a happy, peaceful and stress controlled life. My ideas to bring mental health awareness are to introduce people to the this concept by organising workshops and camps. Most people are not aware that they have mental health issues and they continue to live in stress induced ,unhappy life. They convince themselves that what we are going through is part and parcel of life, however they do not realise there are ways to control it and how having mental health issues affects you entire internal system in the long run. I feel mental health awareness must be introduced to people at a young age. The amount of competition is tremendously increasing , youngsters are burnt out when they come back from school. Inculcating knowledge and practice of good ways to have mental health in control must start at a young age for a better future.
